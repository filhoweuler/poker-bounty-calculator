About the Calculator

We use a simple math strategy to create an equivalence between bounty and chip count. Basically we say that the initial bounty is worth half initial stack. Therefore if the target has 10x the initial bounty we'll add 10x half of the initial stack to the pot and calculate the equity needed for a breakeven call.

Although this is a very well known strategy for calculation, it's not very acurate on some situations and alternatives exist.
